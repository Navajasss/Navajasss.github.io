IRON FARM

Operation

This farm is known for the gigantic amount of iron it produces, as it collects up to 4000+/h

The way this farm works is through a mechanic within the game that involves the villagers. This mechanic focuses on the villages and the golems, since if there are scared villagers within a village, they will generate an iron golem to try to defend themselves from the mobs. We will use this to our advantage, making mini villages within a sector to scare the villagers from time to time so that they generate iron golems, to later kill them and they will give us their loot.

Materials for the farm

Tips to keep in mind

- When making the farm, try not to be near a village or villagers that are not from the farm, since they can take the beds of others and can influence the spawning of golems.

- be at least 30 blocks away from the ground, otherwise the golems will spawn outside the farm


MOB FARM

Operation

This farm generates "infinite" amounts of loot resources from mobs.
It works by using darkness to spawn hostile mobs and kill them and get their drop automatically. It uses a redstone clock mechanism, which every so often puts water on the platforms to push the mobs out, and every other time, it takes out the water to make the mobs appear. All this constantly.

Materials for the farm

Tips to keep in mind

- Make the farm in a flat place or in the sea, so the mobs will focus on spawning inside your farm and not outside.

- Make a platform +50 blocks above the roof of your farm, it will be your AFK zone

- Light or Slabear outside the farm so your farm will be more effective
